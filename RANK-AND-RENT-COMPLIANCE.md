# Rank and Rent Compliance Guidelines

## Core Rules

### Never Claim Past Projects or Specific Clients
- Do not reference specific past projects or client names
- Frame scenarios as examples of the type of engagement we can handle
- Invite contact to share real references upon request

### No Fabricated Information
- Do not fabricate timelines or outcomes
- Do not make up specific results or success stories
- Use only verifiable information

### No Testimonials or Reviews
- No testimonials from clients
- No review stars or ratings
- No badges or certifications without verifiable sources

### Tax and Legal Disclaimers
- 1031 defers income tax on qualifying real property
- 1031 does not remove transfer or documentary taxes
- Educational content only, not tax, legal, or investment advice
- Always include appropriate disclaimers

### Securities Disclaimers
- If DST or TIC is mentioned, include a securities disclaimer
- We do not sell securities
- We provide introductions to licensed providers only

### Footer Disclosure
All pages must include this disclosure:

```
Disclosure: This site routes inquiries to our chosen fulfillment partner for 1031 exchange advisory and property identification support.
```

### Content Standards
- Use plain English
- No contractions
- No em dashes
- Technical accuracy required
- Do not make up information about locations
- Verify all location specific details

### Example Framing
When describing capabilities, use this format:

```
Example of the type of engagement we can handle:
[Service Type] in [Location]
Situation: [General scenario]
Our Approach: [How we would handle]
Expected Outcome: [General expected result]
Contact CTA: Contact us to discuss your situation in [Location]. We can share references upon request.
```

## Enforcement
All content must be reviewed against these guidelines before publication.
